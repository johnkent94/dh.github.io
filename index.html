About

YAI

D and H foundation, is a subsidiary of YOUTH AFLAME INTERNATIONAL (YAI), which is a Non-Governmental Organization(NGO), that has existed since 2011, and consist of youths driven with the passion for transforming and re-orientating young minds on the values and disciplines that make for the laudable change we all desire in our society. We have organised various campaigns and conferences in Nigeria.
